Rules and Guidelines for VOTERS



1.All voters must have their Aadhaar Card along with them,without which you cant vote.

2.Please keep checking the announcement page regularly as the registration,voting and results dates are time to time updated here.

3.The whole system employs a Unique (Aadhaar & Email) System i.e 1 Email=1 Aadhaar no.

4.The mail id entered by the user is going to be used as a medium for all future communication,
so please ensure that the mail accountis functioning and log in once to make sure everything is in order.

5.Indian Citizens residing in other countries should make a note of the difference in the timezones and the format of the time mentioned in the site.(GMT,etc).

6.Voters can vote multiple times but,the last casted vote would be considered in the result.
