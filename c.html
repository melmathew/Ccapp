Rules and Guidelines for CANDIDATES



1)The candidate must possess all the valid documents including his/her Aadhaar card,else he/she cant stand for election.

2)The candidate registration dates will be displayed in the announcement page.Please check this page regularly.

3)A candidate can also cast his vote by registering as a voter.

4)The candidate should have a functioning email account as the password/UniqID will be communicated through that ID.

5)On the date of the results the candidate should check the announcements page.
