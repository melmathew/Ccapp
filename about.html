  • WHY DO WE NEED A ONLINE ELECTION SYSTEM?

    India has the largest democracy in the world, with a population of over 1.3 billion people and conducting an election for such a large country is a tremendous job. Every time over $300 million is spent for conducting election and over lakhs of people and police men are kept on duty. Elect_net is an online platform introduced specially for the election purposes which includes the details about the voters, candidates and various details about election.

  • That's COOL...But Are there any advantages of this whole "everything online" process

    - - - PROS - - -


    • Reduces cost for the election over a great extent.


    • People can cast their votes from anywhere.


    • People can vote without any kind of pressure from the candidates who are standing for the election.


    • A person need not stand in long queue for casting their votes
